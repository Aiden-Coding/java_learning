####根据年龄查询
GET /mymayikt/user/_search?q=age:21
####查询年龄在30岁到60岁
GET /mymayikt/user/_search?q=age[30 TO 60]
####查询年龄在30岁到60岁,并且排序，并且分页查询
GET /mymayikt/user/_search?q=age[30 TO 60]&sort=age:desc&from=0&size=2
